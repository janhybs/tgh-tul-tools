ARRMERGE Merge two sorted arrays

You get two sorted lists of integer numbers on the input. The output should by a sorted list of all numbers in the input lists (dulicities included).

Input

First line: two numbers N and M, sizes of the input lists.
Next N lines: the first list
Following M lines: the second list

Output

N+M lines with numbers of the resulting list

Example


Input:
3 5
1
3
7
2
5
6
8
9 

Output:
1
2
3
5
6
7
8
9
Limits
Time limit
60s

All
