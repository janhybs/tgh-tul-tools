RELCONN Reliable connection

Consider an radio network of consisting of N stations. Some pairs of stations are connected by an wireless connection of various relaibility. You have to write a program that finds most reliable route from the station A to the station B. Obviously, the probability of successfull transmition through the route is a product of probabilities of no error on individual connection on the route.

Input

There is the number of stations N, and number of connections M on the first line. On the following M lines there are data of individual connections, two indeces of connected stations and the real number of the probability of success on the connection. On the line M+2 there is number of test cases K followed by K lines with pairs of stations for which the route should by found.

Output

K lines with lists of stations on resulting most reliable routes including the end stations.

Example


Input:
3 3
0 1 0.8
0 2 0.5
1 2 0.7
1
0 2

Output:
0 1 2
